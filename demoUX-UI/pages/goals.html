Expand Research

Extend supermarket research to 20+ locations across multiple cities in Japan, creating a comprehensive national database of packaging practices.

Reach 1000 signatures on our Change.org petition and present findings directly to major supermarket chains and government officials.

Corporate Partnerships

Establish formal partnerships with at least 3 supermarket chains to pilot plastic-free initiatives and sustainable packaging alternatives.

Education Program
