Our Advocacy Approach

We believe in evidence-based advocacy that balances environmental goals with business realities. Through direct engagement with supermarkets and businesses, we work to identify practical solutions for reducing plastic waste while maintaining operational efficiency.

Our approach combines research-backed recommendations with an understanding of the challenges businesses face. We don't just point out problems â€“ we collaborate on implementable solutions that work for both the environment and business operations.

From manager interviews to corporate-level discussions, we advocate for policies and practices that make sustainable packaging the easy choice, not the difficult one.

Key Advocacy Areas

Supermarket Partnerships

Direct engagement with supermarket managers and corporate offices to advocate for reduced plastic packaging, reusable alternatives, and transparent recycling systems.

Policy Advocacy

Working with local government and policymakers to support regulations that reduce single-use plastics while providing businesses with adequate transition periods.

Consumer Education

Empowering consumers to make informed choices and advocate for change through their purchasing decisions and direct feedback to retailers.

Industry Best Practices

Researching and promoting successful plastic reduction initiatives from other regions and industries to show what's possible and achievable.
