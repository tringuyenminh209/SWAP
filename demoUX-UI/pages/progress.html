Our Journey

JANUARY 2024

SWAP Initiative Founded

Group of high school students came together with a shared vision to tackle plastic waste in Japanese supermarkets. Initial planning and research phase began.

FEBRUARY 2024

First Supermarket Survey

Conducted comprehensive survey of plastic packaging practices at local supermarkets. Documented findings and identified key areas for improvement.

MARCH 2024

Educational Campaign Launch

Launched first awareness campaign at school. Organized workshops and presentations to educate students about plastic waste impacts.

APRIL 2024

Petition Campaign Started

Launched Change.org petition calling for reduced plastic packaging in supermarkets. Reached 50 signatures within first week.

MAY 2024

Partnership with Toyonaka City Hall

Established partnership with Toyonaka City Hall environmental department. Gained official support for our initiatives.

JUNE 2024

Expanded Research to 6+ Locations

Completed comprehensive research across 6+ supermarket locations in the area. Published detailed findings and recommendations.

PRESENT

Continuing Our Mission

Reached 100+ petition signatures, 1000+ people impacted. Working on expanding partnerships and implementing sustainable solutions.
