Our Mission

SWAP (Stop Waste And Plastic) is a student-led initiative dedicated to reducing plastic waste in Japanese supermarkets. We believe that sustainable change starts at the local level, and through advocacy, education, and collaboration, we can create a future where unnecessary plastic packaging is replaced with eco-friendly alternatives.

Our mission is to work directly with supermarkets, educate our communities about the environmental impact of plastic waste, and build partnerships that lead to practical, sustainable solutions. Together, we can make a difference‚Äîone grocery trip at a time.

What We Do

Advocate

We work with supermarkets and businesses to reduce single-use plastic packaging and promote sustainable alternatives.

Educate

Through workshops and campaigns, we raise awareness about the environmental impact of plastic waste in our communities.

Collaborate

We partner with local organizations, schools, and businesses to create practical solutions for reducing plastic waste.
