Our Mission

Our organization's primary goal is to tackle the growing issue of plastic waste originating from packaging in Japanese supermarkets. By promoting sustainable alternatives and raising awareness about the environmental impact of single-use plastics, we aim to inspire change at both the consumer and corporate levels.

As a high school student-led initiative, we are dedicated to collaborating with local communities, schools, and businesses to implement creative, practical solutions that reduce waste and contribute to a cleaner, greener future for Japan.

What We Do

Advocate

We work with supermarkets and businesses to reduce single-use plastic packaging and promote sustainable alternatives.

Educate

Through workshops and campaigns, we raise awareness about the environmental impact of plastic waste in our communities.

Collaborate

We partner with local organizations, schools, and businesses to create practical solutions for reducing plastic waste.
